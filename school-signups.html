Why Join MechMayhem?

This turns robotics into real educational value.

Students do more than watch a STEM demonstration. They design, build, test, troubleshoot, and compete with a robot they helped create.

  • Hands-on STEM learning beyond theory
  • Real engineering design process
  • Teamwork, problem solving, and creativity
  • Confidence with tools and technology
  • Connection with university mentors
  • An exciting competition students remember
