What is MechMayhem?

MechMayhem is an educational robotics program designed to help students move beyond exposure and into real technical capability. Teams build robots, make design choices, test ideas, and compete in a supportive engineering environment.

Photos from Previous Years

MechMayhem launched in 2025 with 2 schools and over 100 people in attendance. We are excited to grow the program in 2026 and beyond!
